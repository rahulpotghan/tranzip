HOW IT WORKS

Tranzip is built upon social logistics resulting in cheaper and faster way to send your packages. Packages can be tracked in real time, eliminates the annoying trip to local shipping store & saves on stationary. All packages are insured to give you peace of mind. Using our Tranzip app one can send packages in a simple, social & faster way across city, state or country. Package transporters (aka Zippers) can find a way to earn $$ during their travel which they are making anyways. Its fun and simple!

Our beautifully designed app helps sender to enter package details, pickup location and destination. Once submitted, package is picked up by “Zippers” who will be carrying your package. These zippers are traveling to your package’s destination anyways on their personal or business trip, why not deliver the package for you, and save you some $$ and at the same time “Zipper” also earns $$ whilst delivering your package. It's very easy to become a Sender or Zipper or both. Tranzip is FREE to use, Try it today!
